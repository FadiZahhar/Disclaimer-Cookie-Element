What Happens on Accept or Decline

On Accept: You can proceed to use cookies as planned. The acceptCookies function sets a consent cookie and hides the disclaimer. You can also initialize any analytics or services that use cookies here.

On Decline: You should respect the user's choice and avoid setting any non-essential cookies. The declineCookies function also sets a consent cookie (indicating decline) and hides the disclaimer. Make sure to disable any non-essential scripts or trackers that use cookies.

Remembering User Preference

The cookieConsent cookie is used to remember the user's choice. If this cookie is present, the disclaimer won't be shown again. This is checked in the window.onload function.
